# Kairos Code of Conduct

Kairos is a public research and website collaboration space for macro-cycle learning.

We welcome disagreement, source correction, counterevidence, and alternative periodization. Strong disagreement is useful when it stays specific, source-aware, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful toward other contributors.
- Critique claims, sources, and reasoning rather than people.
- Separate evidence from interpretation.
- Mark uncertainty clearly.
- Preserve Kairos guardrails around K6 Candidate, Watchlist items, and contested claims.
- Avoid investment advice, trading recommendations, and unsupported macro predictions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Repeatedly presenting unsupported claims as confirmed facts.
- Treating Watchlist items as confirmed facts.
- Treating AI or any other candidate as confirmed K6 without broad evidence.
- Posting private notes, copyrighted excerpts, raw internal Evidence Packs, or material you do not have the right to contribute.
- Spam, promotional content, or off-topic market calls.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, label, close, hide, or remove contributions that violate these expectations.

Maintainers may also ask contributors to restate a claim with clearer sources, softer uncertainty language, or better claim-source mapping before review continues.
